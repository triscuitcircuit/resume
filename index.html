Statement

I am also interested in the current (2021) technology space within Maine. Compared to other states in the area of similar size, Maine does not have much of an offering for jobs in the technology industry. Technology is an ever improving field of study, and compared to other states, Maine seems to be lacking in the companies that area for jobs. This is an issue since students going to college in Maine for a technology focused degree are forced to another state for employment. As more and more devices require technical know-how to operate, this could limit Maine's growth economically and socially. Just recently has a boom in this area started within Maine( mainebiz ), but more has to be done in order for jobs have to stay.
